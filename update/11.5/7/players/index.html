Tier VII player stats

This page list statistics of players’ battles at tier VII during update 11.5.

Battles per Player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 11.5.

On average, players played 32 battles. Median was 12 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 11.5 was 4 046 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
