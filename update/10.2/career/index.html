Career stats

Career stats

This page shows statistics of players’ battles fought during their Blitz “career” until the end of update 10.2. In total, 1.81M players played during update 10.2.

Battles per Player

Number of career/cumulative battles per player in the end of the update 10.2.

An average player has played 3824 battles by the end of update 10.2. Median battle count was 499 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) was 272k battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update (i.e. “90% most inactive” == “10% most active”).
