Career stats

This page shows statistics of players’ battles fought during their Blitz “career” until the end of update 11.6. In total, 2.01M players played during update 11.6.

Battles per Player

Number of career/cumulative battles per player in the end of the update 11.6.

An average player has played 3528 battles by the end of update 11.6. Median battle count was 439 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) was 305k battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update (i.e. “90% most inactive” == “10% most active”).
