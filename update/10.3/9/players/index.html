Tier IX player stats

This page list statistics of players’ battles at tier IX during update 10.3.

This graph shows players WR at the tier IX during the update 10.3 vs. players’ career battles.

Battles per Player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 10.3.

On average, players played 58 battles. Median was 23 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 10.3 was 9 307 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
